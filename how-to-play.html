How To Play

Introduction Create your Team Managing Your Team Account Balance

MEGASCORES is India’s biggest Sports Game with an exponentially growing user base of over 2 crore sports fans.

MEGASCORES is played by using your sport knowledge and skill. You can pick your own team made up of real players for Cricket, Football, NBA. Create your team within a maximum budget of 100 credits. Your team earns points based on your chosen players' performance in the real-life matches. It's time to showcase your skill and go for glory!

Follow these 5 easy steps to get started:

  • Select A Match:
    Select any of the upcoming matches from any of the current or upcoming cricket series

  • Create Your Team:
    Use your sports knowledge and showcase your skills to create your MEGASCORES team within a budget of 100 credits

  • Join a Contest:
    Join any MEGASCORES free or cash contest to win cash and the ultimate bragging rights to showoff your improvement in the Free/Skill contests on MEGASCORES!

  • Follow the Match:
    Watch the real match and track your fantasy scorecard (updated every 2 minutes)

  • Withdraw your Winnings:
    Instantly withdraw your winnings from your MEGASCORES account (One Time Verification required)

Step 1: Select a Match

Select any upcoming match from the list of ongoing and upcoming cricket series and click on the ‘Create Team’ button.

Create your MEGASCORES team by picking 11 players as per the following combinations (C1,C2,C3...) within a budget of 100 credits.

Player type Minimum Maximum
WK 1 4
Bat 3 6
AR 1 4
Bowl 3 6

Step 2: Create your Team

Select players for your MEGASCORES team from all 4 of the categories mentioned below:

  • WK - Wicket-keeper

  • BAT - Batsmen

  • BWL - Bowlers

  • AR - All-rounders

Check player info by clicking on the player image.

Sort players as per their teams, credits or points.

Keep an eye on:

  • Number of players added to your team

  • Available credits

  • The deadline for team submission

Step 3: Select your MEGASCORES team’s Captain & Vice Captain

After creating your MEGASCORES team, choose a Captain & Vice Captain for the team.

  • Captain - Gets 2x points scored by him in the actual game

  • Vice Captain - Gets 1.5x points scored by him in the actual game

Step 4: Join Contest

Join any MEGASCORES free or cash contest to win cash and the ultimate bragging rights to showoff your improvement in the Free/Skill contests on MEGASCORES!

Step 5: Creating multiple teams

You can create upto 11 teams per match and choose to join a contests with any of the teams created!

Click on 'CREATE TEAM' for creating next team

You can make as many changes to your MEGASCORES teams as you like until the deadline of that match!

You can also change your Captain or Vice Captain before the deadline of the match. Select the “Edit Team” button to make changes to your team.

Make sure you keep an eye on which of your players are playing the match and keep your session updated at all times.

You can view, withdraw or deposit money into your MEGASCORES account anytime by going to the “My Account” link.

You will need to go through a One Time Account Verification process before you withdraw any money from your MEGASCORES account. Don’t worry – this process wont take time and will not be repeated unless you change any of your details.

Money in your MEGASCORES account is divided into 3 categories:

Unutilized: If you have deposited any amount but have not yet joined any league using this amount, it will be categorized as Unutilized in your MEGASCORES account

Winnings: The amount of money earned as “winnings” through any cash leagues joined. No processing fees will be deducted if you wish to withdraw any amount from your Winnings!

Cash Bonus: It’s on us! This is given by MEGASCORES to you, and can be used to join any public cash leagues and win more cash! This cash bonus comes with an expiry date so make full use of this freebie and get started!
